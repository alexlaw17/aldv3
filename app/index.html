About me

I'm a graphic and web/UI designer based in New York City. I enjoy crafting pixel perfect designs for the web and print. Web usability is very important to me, and I specialize in creating responsive websites and working on the Adobe Suite.

During my free time I like to work on vector graphics, drawing, and hand lettering. I'm passionate about my field of work, easy to talk to, and I love listening to peoples' interests.
