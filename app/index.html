About

I'm a visual designer based in New York City with an interest in art, technology, and design. I enjoy creating beautiful experiences for the web. Drawing, exercising, and reading comic books are a few of my favorite pastime. I'm passionate about what I do, easy to talk to, and I love listening to peoples' interests.

If you would like to know more about me, or the work that I do, feel free to send me a message below in the contact section. I look forward to hear from you.
